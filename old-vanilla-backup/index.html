About

A Career Dedicated to Safer Medicine

Prof. Dr. Azza Baraka is a Professor of Clinical Pharmacology at Alexandria University's Faculty of Medicine, where she has spent over two decades shaping how future physicians understand and prescribe medications. Her work sits at the intersection of academic research, medical education, and real-world patient safety.

As Director of the Quality Assurance Unit, she leads institution-wide efforts to raise the bar for medical education standards. Beyond the lecture hall, she's a published researcher, a mentor to hundreds of medical students, and a vocal advocate for evidence-based prescribing practices.
